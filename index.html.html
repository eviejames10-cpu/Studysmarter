A note from the creator

You might expect this website to be made by someone who gets all 9s in her mocks — but I'm actually a student who's spent a long time feeling lost in the middle, watching everyone else seem to just know how to revise. Having ADHD makes it even harder. So I built this because I wanted a place where students like me can find the right tool for them — no sign-up, no cost, no overwhelm. Just honest recommendations.
